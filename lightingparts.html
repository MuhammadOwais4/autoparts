Lighting parts



A car lighting part, often referred to as a lighting component or automotive lighting fixture, is an interchangeable part that is kept in inventory and used for the repair or replacement of defective lighting systems in vehicles. Car lighting parts are crucial for vehicle safety and visibility, playing a significant role in logistics engineering and supply chain management, often involving dedicated lighting parts management systems.
These parts are a result of advancements in automotive technology and mass production techniques.
