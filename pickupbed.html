Pickup bed



A pickup bed, also known as a truck bed, is an interchangeable part that is kept in an inventory and used for the repair or replacement of damaged or defective truck beds. Pickup beds are an important feature of automotive design and functionality,
often comprising dedicated pickup bed management systems within the automotive supply chain.

Pickup beds are a result of advancements in automotive manufacturing and design, contributing to the versatility and utility of pickup trucks.
